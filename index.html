Democracy Risk Score
The Democracy Risk Score is a composite 0–100 index computed in real time from multiple signal categories. A score of 0 represents a fully functioning democracy with no detected threats; 100 represents complete democratic failure. Scores above 60 trigger elevated ELECTCON alert levels.

ELECTCON Alert Levels
ELECTCON (Election Condition) mirrors defense readiness models. Level 5 (Normal) indicates standard democratic operations. Levels 4–3 indicate elevated monitoring and increased threat activity. Levels 2–1 indicate critical threats to electoral integrity requiring immediate public attention.
Data Sources
All intelligence is gathered via live AI-assisted web search (Claude AI · Anthropic), cross-referenced against ProPublica investigative reporting, Democracy Docket litigation tracking, the Brennan Center for Justice, and official U.S. State Election Authority records. Scores update continuously as new information becomes available.
